* Mass centroid
Formula for the mass centroid on the x and y axes,
$$x_c = \frac{\sum_{i = 1}^n x_i m_i}{\sum_{i = 1}^n m_i}$$
$$y_c = \frac{\sum_{i = 1}^n y_i m_i}{\sum_{i = 1}^n m_i}$$
